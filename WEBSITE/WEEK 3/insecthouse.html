INSECT HOUSE

The insect house is home to a variety of insects, showcasing their diversity and importance in ecosystems. It provides a unique opportunity to observe these fascinating creatures up close.

Insects play crucial roles in pollination, decomposition, and as a food source for other animals. The insect house features various species, including butterflies, beetles, and ants, each contributing to the ecosystem in its own way.
