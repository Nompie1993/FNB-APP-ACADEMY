ANIMALS MAP PLACES

ELEPHANTS

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence and strong social bonds.

GEMSBOK

Gemsbok are large antelopes known for their long, straight horns and striking appearance.

GIRAFFES

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks and unique coat patterns.

GORILLAS

Gorillas are the largest primates, known for their intelligence and strong family bonds.

KOALAS

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia, known for their tree-dwelling habits and eucalyptus diet.

LIONS

Lions are known as the kings of the jungle, famous for their majestic manes and social pride structure.

MONKEYS

Monkeys are intelligent primates known for their playful behavior and social structures.

PANDAS

Pandas are known for their distinctive black and white fur and are native to China.

TIGERS

Tigers are the largest cat species, known for their powerful build and striking orange coat with black stripes.

WARTHOGS

Warthogs are wild pigs known for their distinctive tusks and rugged appearance.
