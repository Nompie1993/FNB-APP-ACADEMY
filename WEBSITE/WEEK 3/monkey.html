MONKEYS

Monkeys are a group of primates that are typically characterized by their long tails, social behavior, and high intelligence. They are found in various habitats around the world, including tropical rainforests, savannas, and mountains.

Monkeys are divided into two main groups: New World monkeys, which are found in the Americas, and Old World monkeys, which are found in Africa and Asia. They exhibit a wide range of behaviors, including tool use, communication, and complex social structures.
