TIGERS

Tigers are the largest species of the cat family, known for their distinctive orange coat with black stripes. They are native to Asia and are primarily found in forests, grasslands, and wetlands.

Tigers are solitary hunters and are known for their strength and agility. They primarily hunt large prey such as deer, wild boar, and buffalo. Tigers are also excellent swimmers and often take to water to cool off or hunt.
