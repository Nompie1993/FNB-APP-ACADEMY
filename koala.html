KOALAS

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia, known for their tree-dwelling habits and eucalyptus diet. They are often recognized for their large, round faces and fluffy ears.

Koalas are primarily nocturnal and spend most of their time sleeping in eucalyptus trees. They have a specialized diet, feeding almost exclusively on eucalyptus leaves, which are toxic to most other animals. Koalas have a unique digestive system that allows them to detoxify the chemicals in the leaves.
